About

Koubouratou Idjaton is a scientist specialised in applied research in Artificial Intelligence and machine learning. She has over a decade experiences in software engineering, data and AI. Trained in Benin, the USA and France, she holds a PhD in Artificial Intelligence from the University of Orleans, an Executive certificate in Business Administration from Kellogg Northwestern University and a Master of Science in Computer Science from the University of Abomey-Calavi. Her experience has been working as an expert on several AI and Data projects building real world solutions with AI. Her work combines research and concrete applications for products in a variety of sectors: industry, administration, environment, aeronautics and cultural heritage. With a pronounced engagement for education and knowledge sharing, she teaches in Polytech, for business professionals and executives; and in online teaching and mentoring through OpenClassrooms.

Short Story

Born in Porto-Novo, the capital of Benin, Dr. IDJATON Koubouratou Olowountogni grew up between Lokossa and Cotonou, two cities that shaped her identity and broadened her vision. She is a passionate advocate for gender equality, youth engagement, and the promotion of science and technology. The daughter of a mathematician and a devoted artisan mother, Dr. IDJATON inherited both scientific rigor and artistic creativity. Her diverse cultural background instilled in her a deep respect for others, a spirit of tolerance, and a strong belief in continuous learning as a path to better serve humanity.

Vision

Dr. IDJATON envisions a world where technology and innovation are accessible to all, regardless of their background. She believes in the power of education to transform lives and is committed to empowering the next generation of scientists, engineers, and innovators, particularly women and underrepresented minorities in STEM fields. Through her work, she aims to bridge the gap between cutting-edge research and practical applications that address real-world challenges, fostering a more inclusive and equitable technological landscape.

Services

I support you in your projects to implement innovative solutions that enable you to make the most of your data and gain a competitive advantage. I work across the entire data workflow, from extraction to production, including modeling. Beyond my technical and functional expertise, my interpersonal skills have enabled me to collaborate efficiently with teams from different backgrounds and cultures. Applied AI Project consulting

Design, implement, and optimize AI/Machine Learning models to solve specific business or research problems. The consulting consist on transforming raw data into actionable, reliable AI solutions. It turns your AI ambition into results.

GenAI, RAG and AI Agent development

Build and deploy GenAI models and AI Agent systems that automate complex, multi-step tasks for your business. This is beyond simple chatbots, you get intelligent workflows that perform actions, interact with external systems, and deliver valuable assets.

Executive AI Literacy

Deliver high-level, tailored workshop and training sessions on AI technologies to C-suite and leadership teams to clarify the strategic impact, risks, and opportunities. This helps leadership make informed strategic decisions about AI adoption and investment.

Technical due diligence for AI Products

Provides an expert assessment of the technical feasibility and long-term scalability of an AI-focused startup or product for investors or acquiring companies. The review focuses on the underlying algorithms, data quality, and engineering practices.

AI and DATA audit

Evaluate a business or organization's existing technology stack, data pipeline, and data governance for AI readiness. It results in a roadmap for collecting, storing, and securing data. Uses cases to utilize those data to support future AI initiatives efficiently are also proposed.

Responsible AI and ethics review

Reviews AI systems for bias, fairness, transparency, and compliance with emerging ethical standards and regulations. This contributes to ensuring AI deployment is trustworthy and minimizes legal or reputational risk.
